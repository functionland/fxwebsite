Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical or Web3 knowledge?

Not at all. You just download the app from Google Play or the App Store and use it like any normal app. Everything decentralized happens behind the scenes.

What makes this different from Google Drive or iCloud?

Your files are stored on a decentralized network instead of a single company's servers. You truly own your data — no one can access, mine, or shut off your storage.

Is my data safe on the Fula network?

Yes. Your data is encrypted and replicated across multiple nodes. It's actually more resilient than traditional cloud storage because there's no single point of failure.

What apps can I use right now?

FxFiles is available on Android and iOS for file storage and photo backup. More apps are being built by developers on the Fula network every day.

Do I need to know Web3 to build an app?

No. Fula is based on standard S3 APIs, so you can use the same libraries and tools you already know. No blockchain or Web3 knowledge required.

Can I migrate my existing app to Fula?

Yes. If your app already supports S3, migrating is as simple as changing the S3 endpoint. No rewrite needed — just point to the Fula network and you're done.

Can I build apps for any platform?

Yes. Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux all have S3 libraries available for app developers. Build once with standard APIs, deploy everywhere.

What's the benefit for developers?

Storage is more reliable thanks to built-in replication, and data storage costs are offloaded from your infrastructure. You can focus on the app experience instead of dealing with storage costs and complications.

Do I need technical skills to run FxBlox?

Not at all. FxBlox is plug-and-play. Connect it to your network and storage, and the one-click setup handles the rest.

How much can I earn with FxBlox?

Earnings depend on your storage contribution, uptime, and network demand. The more reliable your node and the more storage you provide, the more FULA rewards you earn.

What internet speed do I need?

Basic broadband is sufficient. FxBlox works efficiently with standard home internet. A stable connection matters more than raw speed.

How much does it cost to run?

FxBlox is a low-power device that costs under $1 a month in electricity. It only needs air for cooling — no fans, no noise, no high energy bills.
