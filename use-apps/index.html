How can I use the applications?

1

Install

Get the application from your preferred method, either Appstore on iOS, Google Play on Android or other means.

2

Login

Currently login is with Google and Apple, but it does not mean your data is sent to their servers. We only use them to generate your private keys.

3

Get Storage

Currently you need to get Fula tokens to cover the cost of using and storing data on Fula network, which is up to 2 times cheaper than central cloud.

Discover the Advantages of Functionland!

Increased Privacy

Your data remains entirely yours, eliminating central vulnerabilities and ensuring optimal security.

Data is encrypted on-device and split into pieces duplicated across the blockchain network.

Affordable Storage

Storage is significantly cheaper due to low-electricity device operation, eliminating expensive server infrastructure.

Low-power nodes keep operational costs minimal, passing the savings directly to users.
